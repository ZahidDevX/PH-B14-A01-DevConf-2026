Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about DevConf 2026.

Is there a student discount available?

Yes, students with a valid ID receive 40% off the Standard pass. Email us with proof of enrollment to claim your discount code.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to a colleague at no extra cost.

Will sessions be recorded?

All talks are recorded and made available to attendees. Standard pass holders get 30-day access; Pro pass holders get lifetime access.

Is the venue wheelchair accessible?

Yes, the entire venue is fully wheelchair accessible, including ramps, elevators, and accessible restrooms on every floor.

What's included in the swag bag?

Every attendee receives a branded backpack, T-shirt, notebook, stickers, and exclusive sponsor merchandise.

Do you offer group or team discounts?

Yes, our Team pass covers up to 10 people at a discounted rate. Contact our sales team for larger groups.
